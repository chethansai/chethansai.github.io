The Beard

By S Chethan Sai Krishna

Published on 6th July 2019


As he went scratching his beard in distress what more  future is instilled for him
He got reminded the stories that same beard told him
Tears rolling out for noting sadness to vaulting the dropping tear edge in beard hiding.. to stand up on own
A showful Happinessl realised to smiles covered behind beard
Worrying pimples on face to covering them up realising the eternality of beauty
Fearing to world to Twirling moustache to learn to fear world
Chubbying to hands grabing on using it to pulling over onto lips..
Taking dad for haircut to saloon to going alone for trimming the beard.
Cheek kisses of my mom to forehead kisses coz its thorny

It spoke my grief,hid my happiness,shaped my perspective,learnt me strength,built courage, independency,self-sustainity

The stories of beard wetted his beard
