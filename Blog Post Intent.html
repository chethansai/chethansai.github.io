The Beard

   A verse on teenage
     By S Chethan Sai Krishna
Published on 19th August 2019

Scratching his beard in leisure..
Anticipating future..
His own beard fables had at disclosure..

Attention seeking tear drops for stumble falls
TO..,
Beard vaulting tear edges(to stand up on own)

Conviction, considering opinions for pimples
TO...,
Beard shielding pimples(to realize eternality of beauty)

Fearing worlds
TO...,
Twirling moustaches(to learn to fear the world)

Cheek pulls
To...,
Beard-grabbed-pulls onto lips

Dad for haircuts
TO..,
Beard trimmed went alones

Mom cheek kisses..,
TO..,
Beard thorny hence turned forehead kisses

Showful happinesses
TO..,
Behind the Beard hid smiles


It spoke his grief, hid his happiness, shaped his perspective, endowed him strength, built his courage, stood him independent, architected his self-sufficiency


The stories of beard wetted his beard..
